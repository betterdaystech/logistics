Ground Shipping

We provide road freight solutions for both domestic and international door-to-door ground freight transportation. Be it a full truck load or less than a truck load, we provide a clear statement outlining the transit duration and tracking service for your convenience. Whether there is a need for a consolidated or direct road service, our experts are capable of transporting your goods. A variety of options are available for the most competitive and reliable ground freight forwarding service. For freight consignments that require a full truck load, we dedicate a van or truck for the entire move. This being from the shipper’s door or warehouse to when the freight is received by the recipient at the final destination. The type of equipment used, depends on the type of freight being shipped, its weight and dimensions. For shipments requiring less than a trailer load, the freight is consolidated in our contracted vans and transported efficiently to the recipient’s door. road freight Transit times and service availability varies according to the city, the description of the freight, and its weight and volume. We are a flexible Freight Forwarding enterprise focusing on cost-effective and low-risk solutions for our clients. We have vast experience in the Freight forwarding industry because we boast over 10 years of active service in air-freight, sea-freight, road and rail-freight. So, our clients can trust us to provide a hassle-free experience! Clients can be confident that all relevant paperwork and processes are completed expertly and professionally. Our Value-added services include: warehousing services, door-to-door delivery, import and export services and cargo insurance cover. .
